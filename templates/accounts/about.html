About EyeHealth 20-20-20

Hi, I'm building a solution to protect the vision of digital workers worldwide through scientifically-backed eye health tools. This is my personal mission.

My Story

EyeHealth 20-20-20 was born from my personal battle with severe digital eye strain. As a software developer, I was spending 10+ hours daily staring at screens, leading to constant headaches, dry eyes, and blurred vision.

After multiple doctor visits and discovering the 20-20-20 rule, I realized there was no simple, reliable tool to help people like me follow this proven method consistently. That's when I decided to build EyeHealth 20-20-20 - a straightforward web application that makes protecting your vision as simple as clicking "start".

What started as a personal solution has now helped thousands of users reduce their digital eye strain. Every feature I add is based on real user feedback and my own experience of what actually works for busy professionals.

The 20-20-20 Rule

Every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This simple rule, recommended by eye care professionals worldwide, is the foundation of everything we do.

My Mission & Values

I believe that technology should enhance our lives, not harm our health. These values guide every decision I make for this product.

Health First

I prioritize user health over profits. Every feature is designed based on scientific research, medical recommendations, and real user needs.

Accessibility

Eye health tools should be accessible to everyone. That's why EyeHealth 20-20-20 is 100% free with all features unlocked for all users.

Innovation

I continuously improve the app based on user feedback and my own daily use, making eye health habits easier to maintain for busy professionals.
