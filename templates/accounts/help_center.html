Why I'm Passionate About Eye Health

The science behind the 20-20-20 rule and why I believe it can change your daily comfort.

What I Learned About Eye Strain

After years of 12-hour coding days, I learned that prolonged screen time reduces blink rate and exhausts our focusing muscles.

Why 20-20-20 Changed My Life

Looking at distant objects for 20 seconds every 20 minutes relaxes focusing muscles and encourages natural blinking. Simple, but it works.

The Results I've Seen

Regular breaks have eliminated my daily headaches, reduced dry eyes, and helped me work longer with comfort. Users report similar results.
