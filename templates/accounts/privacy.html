At EyeHealth 20-20-20, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our web application and services.

Quick Summary: We only collect essential information to provide our service, never sell your data to third parties, and you have full control over your information.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address (required for login)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Password (encrypted and never stored in plain text)

Usage Information

To improve our service, we automatically collect:

  • Timer session data (start/end times, break durations)
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and general location (country/region)

Premium Features Data

For Premium subscribers, we additionally store:

  • Custom timer preferences
  • Analytics and progress history
  • Achievement and streak data

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision
  • Provide timer functionality
  • Save your preferences
  • Generate progress reports
  • Send break reminders
Service Improvement
  • Analyze usage patterns
  • Fix bugs and issues
  • Develop new features
  • Optimize performance

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share limited information only in these circumstances:

Service Providers

We work with trusted third parties who help us operate our service:

  • Cloud Hosting: Secure data storage and processing
Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and users.
